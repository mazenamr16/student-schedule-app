Our Story

From a small kitchen to your favorite table

Founded in 2018, Saffron Table began as a humble dream in a small kitchen with a big heart. What started as family dinners shared with friends quickly grew into something more — a place where people could come together over food made with genuine love and care.

Our passion for authentic flavors drives everything we do. We believe that every dish should tell a story, honoring traditional recipes while embracing the creativity of modern cooking. From aromatic spices to slow-simmered sauces, we pour our hearts into every plate that leaves our kitchen.

We are committed to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients in every meal we prepare. Combined with our warm hospitality, we strive to make every guest feel like they are dining at home. At Saffron Table, you are not just a customer — you are part of our family.

Chef Marco Rivera

Head Chef & Founder

Chef Marco grew up watching his grandmother cook in a bustling kitchen filled with the aromas of cumin, saffron, and fresh herbs. Those childhood memories sparked a lifelong passion for culinary arts that led him to train at some of the finest kitchens across Europe and Latin America.

After years of honing his craft, Marco returned home to open Saffron Table — a restaurant that brings together everything he learned on his journey. His philosophy is simple: cook with heart, use the best ingredients, and always make your guests feel welcome.
